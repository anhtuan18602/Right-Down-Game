Experiment Instructions


In this experiment you will make decisions in a game in which you will be matched with other participants.

Your earnings (bonus payments) depend partly on your decisions, partly on the decisions of other participants, and partly on chance.

The Game

Players in this game take decisions one after another in a sequence and are numbered according to their position in the sequence: Player 1, Player 2, Player 3, and so on.

On your turn, you will choose between two actions: right or down.
